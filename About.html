Friendship

Friendship is a special bond between people who care about each other and enjoy spending time together.
Friendships can be important for your health and well-being, and can help you in many ways, including:
Emotional support: Friends can help you cope with hard times, such as divorce, serious illness, job loss, or the death of a loved one.
Mental health: Friendships can help protect kids from mental health issues like anxiety and depression.
Sense of belonging: Friendships can help you feel connected, valued, and purposeful. Happiness: Friendships can boost your happiness and lower your stress.
Self-confidence: Friendships can help improve your self-confidence and feelings of self-worth. Healthy habits: Friendships can encourage you to change or avoid unhealthy habits.
